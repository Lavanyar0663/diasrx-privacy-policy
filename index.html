Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026

DIAS Rx is committed to protecting the privacy and security of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when using the DIAS Rx application.

Information We Collect

DIAS Rx may collect and process information required for healthcare and pharmacy workflow management, including:

  • User account information such as name, email address, phone number, role, and login credentials.
  • Patient-related information such as name, age, gender, contact details, and prescription data.
  • Prescription, medication, and pharmacy workflow information required for app functionality.
  • Technical information necessary for security, troubleshooting, and service improvement.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Authenticate users and provide secure access to the app.
  • Manage patients, prescriptions, medication records, and pharmacy workflows.
  • Improve system performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Maintain security, monitor misuse, and comply with legal or institutional requirements.

Data Sharing

DIAS Rx does not sell personal data. Information may be shared only where necessary for app functionality, institutional use, legal compliance, or authorized administrative and medical workflows.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or misuse.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to operate the application, maintain healthcare or institutional records, and comply with applicable legal obligations.

User Rights

Users may request correction or deletion of their personal data, subject to medical, legal, and institutional record retention requirements.

Children's Privacy

DIAS Rx is intended for authorized institutional and healthcare-related use. It is not designed for direct use by children without authorized supervision.
